Class: Patient

Patient(new_patient)

Primitive class that represents a patient, an actual person. Stores data that typically does not change from encounter to encounter, as well as notes for "frequent fliers" that will be saved independent of encounter. Designed to be extended by browser (frontend) and node.js (backend) specific models
